  • March 14, 1879: Born in Ulm, Germany. A curious mind begins to wonder.
  • 1905: A "Miracle Year"! Publishes groundbreaking theories on relativity and the photoelectric effect, forever changing our understanding of the universe.
  • 1915: Unveils the General Theory of Relativity, explaining gravity as the curvature of spacetime. Mind-blowing!
  • 1921: Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his revolutionary work on the photoelectric effect.
  • 1933: Forced to flee Nazi Germany and finds refuge in the United States.
  • April 18, 1955: A brilliant mind departs, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire generations.
